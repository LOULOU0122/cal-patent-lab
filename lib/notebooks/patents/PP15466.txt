 A new and distinct cultivar of Chrysanthemum plant named ‘Yellow Yowoodstock’, characterized by its uniform and outwardly spreading plant habit; strong and freely branching growth habit; dark green-colored foliage; uniform flowering response and habit; can be grown as a disbud or as a spray-type; early flowering habit; large anemone-type inflorescences; yellow-colored ray florets and enlarged yellow green to yellow-colored disc florets; and good postproduction longevity with plants maintaining good substance and color for about two to three weeks in an interior environment.
