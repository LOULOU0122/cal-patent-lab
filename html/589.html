Welcome to the USPTO e-FOIA page. Through this page you can search for publicly available final decisions rendered by the Patent Trial and Appeal Board.In the field below you can enter a proceeding number to search the collection of available decisions.If you do not have a proceeding number but you know the caption of the case you are looking for, for example ABC Co. v. University of Illinois, or you know a text string found in thecase you can search the collection by using the "Search Document Text" field by typing the text string in the search field.The Final Decision date range starts on July 1, 1997

 
 
 

Search syntax: Use the operators AND, OR, and AND NOT between search terms along with parentheses, e.g. ("john deere" or graham) and not "prior art"
